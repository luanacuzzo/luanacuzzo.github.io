DISCOVERY

The Problem

Thanks to technology, being a writer in today's digital age means that aspiring authors have an alternative to the insanely competitive paradigm of the traditional publishing model.

But, what exactly is wrong with the traditional publishing model?

The Solution

The Online Publishing Model is a combination of print-on-demand hard copy publishing and online digital download. This model has been emerging and it has proved itself to be very efficient in eliminating all the pitfalls of the traditional publishing model. It adds a number of benefits such as the opportunity for small-time authors and publishers to gain more control over the publishing process. It also increases their sales and profits.

The declining costs of self-publishing and the popularity of electronic books will allow many freelance writers to have their work published."

SOURCE: U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, Employment Projections program - Employment projections data for writers and authors, 2018-28

Business Goals

According to Bowker records, Amazonâ€™s market share of self-published print books in the US increased from 6% in 2007 to 92% as of 2018. Amazon's goal is to build centralize an environment to make it possible for people to find whatever they want to buy in one single place. Books have always been of Amazon's largest portion of sales. The Kindle online store represents a high-margin product since it does not require physical operations. The goal is to increase their marketplace supply and, consequently, revenue. It can be done by minimizing the entry barrier for book publishing and by offering a platform that fully supports authors on their self-publishing journey.
